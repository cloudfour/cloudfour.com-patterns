## Maskable PWA and Android Icons

App icons are different sizes and shapes on various Android devices. Some are
square, some are squares with deeply rounded corners, some are circular.
In order to make sure the icon is fully visible with sufficient white space, the
diameter of the icon should be no larger than 80% of the total image height and
width.

Be sure to set the purpose of the icon to "maskable" in your [manifest.json](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Mozilla/Add-ons/WebExtensions/manifest.json) as
well.

[Maskable Icons: Android Adaptive Icons for Your PWA](https://css-tricks.com/maskable-icons-android-adaptive-icons-for-your-pwa/)
