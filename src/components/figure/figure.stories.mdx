# Figure

The `c-figure` class applies styling to the figure and figcaption elements. Figures may be accompanied by an optional caption.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Basic">
    {basicDemo({
      src: 'https://picsum.photos/600/300',
      alt: 'This is an image',
      hasCaption: boolean('Include Caption', true),
    })}
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Styles

The caption styles change with [our dark theme](/?path=/docs/themes--dark).
